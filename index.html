About EKlorix

EKlorix is a pioneering ed-tech and career consultancy firm founded in 2026 in Ranchi, Jharkhand. The company is the vision of its founder and sole owner, Harsh Kumar Singh, who established the platform as a dedicated bridge between academic learning and professional excellence.

Our Origin Story The journey of EKlorix began when Harsh Kumar Singh observed a recurring struggle among his peers and the youth of India. Despite holding degrees, many students found themselves lost in the "gap"—the space between theoretical knowledge and the high-level practical skills demanded by today’s global workforce.

Witnessing this struggle firsthand, Harsh was driven by a single mission: to ensure that no student’s potential is wasted due to a lack of guidance or industry-relevant training. With a deep-seated commitment to empowering the next generation, he founded EKlorix in Ranchi to provide a comprehensive support system that nurtures talent and builds confidence.

Our Mission At EKlorix, our mission is simple: to empower the next generation with strategic guidance and cutting-edge expertise. We provide the full support necessary for students to not just find a job, but to truly thrive in the rapidly evolving digital economy.
